Everything You Need to Know About Universal Studios Singapore Before Your Visit

Enter the world of Jurassic Park, Transformers, Minions and other iconic movies as you hop on exciting rides, enjoy live shows, and eat your way through Universal Studios Singapore.

Fun facts

The Hollywood Zone within Universal Studios contains buildings that are made after actual buildings in Hollywood.

Universal Studios Singapore is one of the two Universal parks to open in South East Asia. The other theme park is Universal Studios Japan, located in Osaka.

This theme park on Sentosa Island covers an area of 62 acres out of the 121 acres that make up Resorts World Sentosa.

About Universal Studios Singapore

  • Universal Studios Singapore is a theme park located inside Resorts World Sentosa on Sentosa Island, Singapore.
  • The park features six themed zones, each with its own attractions, shows, and dining options.
  • Visitors can enjoy exciting rides, including roller coasters and water rides, as well as thrilling experiences based on popular movies and TV shows.
  • The park is home to several immersive experiences, such as Jurassic Park, Transformers, and Battlestar Galactica.
  • Universal Studios Singapore also offers live shows, street performances, and meet-and-greets with beloved characters.
  • With something for everyone, the park is a popular choice for kids, families, groups of friends, and thrill-seekers.

Why visit Universal Studios Singapore?

  • Thrilling Attractions: Universal Studios Singapore offers a range of exciting rides and attractions inspired by popular movies and TV shows such as Transformers, Jurassic Park, and more.
  • Live Entertainment: Experience live shows featuring your favourite characters, stunning special effects and stunning costumes.
  • Immersive-Themed Areas: Explore the park's seven themed zones, each with its own unique atmosphere, design and attractions, giving you a complete and immersive experience.
  • Family-Friendly Fun: The park offers a range of activities and attractions suitable for visitors of all ages, making it a great destination for families.
  • Food and Shopping: Enjoy a variety of food options, from quick bites to sit-down restaurants, and shop for souvenirs and merchandise at the park's many shops and stalls.
  • Entertainment for All Ages: The theme park caters to visitors of all ages with a mix of family-friendly and thrilling attractions, ensuring a fun-filled day for everyone.
  • Convenient Location: The park is located on Sentosa Island, a popular tourist destination in Singapore, making it easily accessible and a convenient choice for a day trip.

Universal Studios Singapore Opening Hours

General Timings: Universal Studios Singapore is open from Monday to Sunday, from 10 AM - 8 PM.

Closed: The theme park is open daily; in case of a maintenance or event happening, they will announce it on their website.

Best Time to Visit: Universal Studios Singapore is very crowded during the weekends. The best time to visit the attraction is on a weekday when the crowd tends to get thinner. But generally speaking, Universal Studios is always lively with crowds irrespective of peak and off peak seasons.

Major Zones at Universal Studios Singapore

Get all set to be transported into a new world at the seven distinctive zones that make up Universal Studios Singapore.

Hollywood

Take a walk on Hollywood Boulevard's Walk of Fame and step into the sets of your beloved blockbuster movies. Here you can have a great time witnessing your favourite movie characters & click photos with them too.

Meet & Greet: Gru & Minions, PO & Master Tigress, Madagascar

Shops: Universal Studios Store, Minion Mart, Hello Kitty Studio Store, The Dark Room,

Restaurants: Mel's drive-in, Pops! Popcorn Delight, Starbucks

New York

Experience the energy of NYC and stroll through its iconic streets, just like you've seen in the movies.

Rides: Sesame Street Spaghetti Space Chase

Shows: Lights Camera Action!, Rhythm Truck

Meet & Greet: Elmo & Oscar the Grooch

Shops: Big Birds Emporium

Restaurants: KT's Grill, Loui's NY Pizza Parlour, Me Want Cookie!

Sci-Fi City

Get a sneak-peek into the future. Hop onto the worlds tallest double roller coaster ride, Battlestar Galactica when you enter this zone.

Rides: Accelerator, Battlestar Galactica: HUMAN VS CYLON, TRANSFORMER the Ride: The Ultimate 3D battle

Meet & Greet: TRANSFORMERS: Voices of Cybertron

Shops: TRANSFORMERS Supply Vault

Restaurants: Frozen Fuel, Planet Yen, Starbot Cafe, Star Dots

Far Far Away

Remember the Kingdom of Far Far Away movie series? This zone is a perfect spot for all the Shrek fans and is a must-visit.

Rides: Enchanted Airways, Magic Potion Spin, Puss in Boots' Giant Journey

Shows: Donkey Live, Shrek 4-D Adventure

Meet & Greet: Happily Ever After (Stars from the Shrek Movie), Puss in the Boots & Kitty Softpaws

Shops: Fairy Godmother's Potion Shop

Restaurants: Friar's Good Food, Goldilocks

The Lost World

Get lost in the world of Jurassic Park and enjoy an action-packed stunt show WaterWorld. Enjoy fun rides like Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure, and Amber Rock Climb.

Rides: Canopy Flyer, Dino Soarin, Jurassic Park Rapid Adventure

Shows: Hatched! featuring Dr Rodney, Rapture Encounter with Blue

Shops: The Dino Store

Restaurants: Discovery Food Court, Fossil Fuel, Jungle Bites

Ancient Egypt

Explore Ancient Egypt and get an insight into a time back in the era. Make sure you try the Revenge of the Mummy roller coaster and Treasure Hunter rides while you are in this zone.

Rides: Revenge of the Mummy, Treasure Hunters

Shows: Lights Camera Action!, Rhythm Truck

Restaurants: Cairo Market, Oasis Spice Cafe, Pharaoh's Dessert Cafe

Frequently asked questions about Universal Studios Singapore

What is Universal Studios Singapore?

A gigantic theme park in Singapore featuring over 20 exciting rides, attractions, and shows spread across 6 different themed zones.

Where is Universal Studios Singapore?

Universal Studios Singapore is situated within the Resorts World Sentosa at Sentosa, Singapore.

What is Universal Studios Singapore famous for?

Universal Studios Singapore is a fun and leisure destination that features themed rides, live shows and attractions in 6 unique themed zones.

Why should I visit Universal Studios Singapore?

Universal Studios Singapore is a great place to let yourself loose and is Southeast Asias only park that offers exhilarating rides with shows and attractions alongside to explore.

How much does it cost to visit Universal Studios Singapore?

The standard entry ticket starts at SGD 92, and gives you full access to all the zones in the park. Combo tickets and express passes will cost higher, depending on the type of ticket chosen.

When did Universal Studios Singapore open?

Universal Studios Singapore opened to the public on 28 May 2011.

Whats inside Universal Studios Singapore?

At Universal Studios Singapore, there are over 20 different rides and attractions to explore, all scattered over 6 themed zones. You can also enjoy street entertainment, grab a great meal, and shop to your hearts content from the gift stores in the park.

What are the Universal Studios Singapore opening hours?

Universal Studio Singapore is open every day from 10 AM to 8 PM. But please note that the hours might vary occasionally and is best to check the hours with the venue before you head out.

Why is Universal Studios Singapore famous?

This is Southeast Asias first Hollywood movie theme park that has a great selection of attractions, entertainment, and rides for families to explore. That, and being located in the popular Sentosa Island off the coast of the mainland, makes it a must-visit attraction while in Singapore.

Is it worth visiting Universal Studios Singapore?

Yes, it is worth visiting Universal Studios Singapore for all the thrilling rides, live shows, immersive experiences, and great food that you get to explore while you are there.
